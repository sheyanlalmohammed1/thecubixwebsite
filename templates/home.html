WHY CUBIX?

The Cubix’s name comes from the impact it hope to leaves on its. The team at The Cubix wants our audience to better understand the complexity of the economic world. Therefore, we decided to name our organization behind the defining aspect of a cube. Every cube has six distinct sides, yet they all come together to create a shape that looks the same from every angle. This is the same for the global community. Every community, country, and individual person may be different but no matter how we look at it, there is a single global community. That is The Cubix!

The Commitment to Progress

100% of Contributions go to Charity

All funds received by The Cubix in the form of payments and donations from the supporting community and private parties will be used to donate to institutions and organizations dedicated to increasing the economic wellbeing of communities in the United States or around the globe

100% Clear Financials

25% of Web-Based Income is used to build The Cubix Network

While 75% of our Ad and Click Revenues are donated to economic Non-Profits, The Cubix uses 25% of its profits to maintain its website, competitive online presence, and expansion through SEO marketing. The Cubix’s end goal is to create as large of an impact on an audience interested in economics and using a portion of income to invest back within our network allows us to do this.
